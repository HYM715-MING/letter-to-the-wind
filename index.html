Wind’s Drift Bottle

Whisper your unspoken thoughts to the wandering wind.

For wandering feelings with no place to go,
let the wind carry them far away.
For unheard voices with no one to listen ,the wind will be there.

Some words are too heavy to say out loud,
and some sorrows find no ear to be heard.
That’s why you’re here now.
Write to the wind — let your drifting,
homeless emotions be carried away to find their journey.

May this be the quietest shelter within your heart.

✉️ Whisper Your Heart to the Breeze
